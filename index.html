Power Lifting Fueled By Plants

No Whey was founded in 2016 by Wayan Swaran and Seren Gwilliam, with the purpose of creating a community space for vegan lifters.

The fitness industry and lifting community often centre excessive meat and dairy consumption as integral to progress.

However, with a growing number of plant-based athletes gaining prominence in their respective fields, this gym seeks to challenge that notion.

Our facilities include state of the art powerlifting equipment to competition standard, as many squat racks as your heart could desire, and machines for accessory movements. We also open our kitchen on weekends to serve up protein-packed vegan goodies!

Whether you're a lifelong vegan, or plant-curious, you're welcome to come and train with and learn from those who are powered by plants.
