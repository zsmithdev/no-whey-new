Classes

Our classes are sorted into three sections, based on ability, plus our youth classes. Sometimes we will also hold one-off classes, such as those in preparations for meets. These will be listed in our events section on our Home page. All our classes center the squat, bench and deadlift as the core moves of any powerlifter. However, we also incorporate accessory lifts, mobility work and discussion of plant-based diet fundamentals in our sessions.


Beginners Class

Learn the basics of the squat, bench and deadlift quickly, safely and effectively. Watch how quickly your strength improves as our coaches guide your progress and our community keeps you motivated.

Every Tuesday and Sunday, 6pm-7.30pm - sign up here

Intermediate Class

If you've aced our beginner classes, or have previous weighlifting experience, intermediate classes will suit you best. Now you have the basics down, it's time to fine tune your form and increase those one rep maxes.

Every Tuesday and Friday, 5.30pm-7pm - sign up here

Expert Class

If you're at competition level and looking to refine your lifts in advance, these are the classes for you. Train with the best as you move weights you never thought possible with hardest working bunch of lifters you'll ever meet.

Every Monday and Thursday, 6pm-8pm - sign up here

Youth Class

For children aged 12 or over, our youth classes teach the basics of strength training at an age-appropriate pace. Activities include movement patterns and light weights as our instructors cater learning to age and ability.

Every Saturday, 12pm-1.30pm - sign up here
